size_t len ) const
overridevirtual

Returns all matched prefixes of a word, sorted by the length (desc).

For example given a dictionary having "a", "an", "b", "ba", "ban", "bana", all the matched prefixes of "banana" are "bana", "ban", "ba", "b".
